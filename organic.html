EXPLORING THE BENEFITS
OF ORGANIC LIVING

 

Scroll Down
 

Expo Commodities Global (Pvt) Ltd practices organic farming and an organic way of processing our spices with a purpose. More than just a business, for us, organic is a concept that creates sustainability throughout our value chain. In other words, “Transparency, fairness, and process management are the key elements that define sustainability.”


Sustainable Organic Process

Farmer Group

All our organic spices are produced and harvested by certified small organic spice farmers across the global value chain. Our farmers are located at the heart of the spice growing regions of each origin where Mother Nature provides the most suitable climatic conditions to grow and harvest the finest authentic spices. We work directly with more than 30,000 small farmers who follow organic, Fairtrade, and sustainable farming practices. Most importantly, we make sure to maintain proper traceability and transparency from the inception of our value chain.

 

Processing

Expo Commodities Global (Pvt) Ltd is certified under globally recognised organic, food safety, and social justice certifications that govern our operations from small farmers to the final export product. We ensure that our day-to-day practices are conducted according to international compliance systems.

Buying Partners

All our Organic products are being analyzed in internationally accredited laboratories and packed according to international standards. Our capability extends to offering customized products ranging from Whole grades, Powder grades and different cut sizes that suit individual customer needs.

Our exports are delivered across multiple global markets in more than 40 different countries.

 

Traceability

Our objectives are not merely to sell products to our customers but to develop the future in terms of human capital, infrastructure, social welfare, financial capital and giving back to society. On the one hand, we develop our stakeholders to the next level preparing them to meet future needs while enabling our systems to create sustainability and give back to Mother Nature.

Without our small farmers, employees, customers, and nature, there is no sustainable LIFE.

“Sustainable Farming - farming that meets the needs of existing and future generations”
