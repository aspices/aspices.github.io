VIETNAMESE CINNAMON ANISE - ASPICE CINNAMON ANISE

Hundreds of years ago, Vietnamese farmers discovered a species of tree with a strong scent and sweet and spicy taste. When I researched and discovered this was one

This is a precious plant, so people brought the seeds to nurse and propagate them until today. That tree is the Cassia Cinnamon tree. The first place to successfully grow Cassia cinnamon in Vietnam is Vien Son Commune, Van Yen, Yen Bai. Due to the suitable soil, the trees here grow very well and contain high essential oil content. Only later did it expand to neighboring areas such as Lao Cai, Quang Ninh, Thanh Hoa... But if the products from the Cinnamon tree have the best quality, it must definitely mention Van Yen Cinnamon grown in Van District. Yen, Yen Bai Province.

The origin of Aspices's cinnamon is grown mainly in communes such as Vien Son, Xuan Ai,... The quality of cinnamon in these areas is very good, with a pungent scent and a sweeter taste than in other areas. Plus Aspices' strict inspection process of input materials will help customers feel extremely secure. We - Aspices say no to mixing substandard products into customer orders. When you have assigned that production responsibility to Aspices, you can rest assured 100% and let us complete the job with all the love and enthusiasm of Aspices.

In the future, Aspices will not stop at cinnamon and anise spices, but we will bring other Vietnamese spices with the best quality. Let's look forward to it.
