Spring Star Anise

Whole Star Anise – Spring harvest has two main qualities. Pick up by hand flowers are called super Spring Star Anise or Tuquy, high flavor and nice colour. Others are called “Canh dan” quality.

Flower size: 2-2.2 cm up (80% up)
 Broken: 7-10% or required
 Moisture: 13.5 % max
 Admixture: 1% max, stemless,

Packing:

  • Carton box Net 5/10 kgs

  • Small packing if required

Origin: Vietnam

Ingredients: 100% natural, do not use any preservatives
