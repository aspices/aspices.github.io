About us

Aspices Co., Ltd is a company that participates directly with farmers from the stages of planting, caring for harvest, preliminary processing, processing, manufacturing and finally exporting into products. The location family types originate 100% in Vietnam. Our guide markets are all countries where the essential need is Cinnamon Anise. Our factory, warehouse and transformation facility are located in Van Yen district, Yen Bai Province, Vietnam. We have a skilled quality control team, right from the stage of input materials, we have chosen extreme control to help customers trust, feel secure and entrust production tasks to me.
