About us

Aspices Co., Ltd is an organization run by Mr. Chinh Nguyen (Edgar Nguyen - Founder of Aspices).

History of the birth of Aspices:

Mr. Chinh Nguyen - From a guy who graduated with a major in Architecture from Hanoi University of Architecture. On a random occasion, during a business trip to Xuan Ai commune, Van Yen district, Yen Bai province. He was awakened by special scents. So he asked the people in that area where the scent came from. Because he was curious, he really wanted to find out where that scent came from. Luckily, he was led into a household by a farmer, where there were pictures of people drying the bark of a certain tree. When he was introduced, he realized it was the Bark of the Cinnamon Tree. That whole day, he was taken to see how people sowed seeds and nursed plants and then to the vast cinnamon forests with large trees. Normally, cinnamon here can be exploited when it is 15 years old. It was dark, he and the group returned to the resort. On the car ride back home, he could not forget the stories about this spice plant. 6 months later, he returned to find that family. Coincidentally, the whole family was going to the forest to exploit cinnamon. In excitement, he asked permission to join his family in mining cinnamon for the whole day. My love for cinnamon grows more and more. Go ahead and turn on your computer and learn about the uses of cinnamon. Cinnamon has many effects on the human body. At a certain moment, he decided to bring Vietnamese cinnamon to the world market. An indispensable spice in many dishes in many countries, or more specifically, there is an extremely famous Vietnamese dish that cannot lack this spice: Pho. He is Chinh Nguyen (Edgar Nguyen) - CEO of Aspices "I want to bring the health benefits of Vietnamese Cinnamon to everyone".

We join hands for a Healthier community, together with Aspices

 

Purpose of ASPICES

Our goal is to build a global spice supply chain, providing the best quality spice products for human health. To do that, we have to build a strict production line, check quality at each stage of production, evaluate daily according to the items on the Test table and immediately fix weak problems. Besides, we always have the principle in production of eliminating defective products immediately after detecting errors. Not for profit but affecting the image and reputation of customers.
